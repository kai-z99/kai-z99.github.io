Rendering Equation

$$ L_o(x, \omega_o) = L_e(x, \omega_o) + \int_{H^2} f_r(x, \omega_i, \omega_o) L_i(x, \omega_i) (\omega_i \cdot n) d\omega_i $$

Separable BSSRDF

$$ S(x_i, \omega_i, x_o, \omega_o) = \frac{1}{\pi} F_r(\omega_i) R(\|x_i - x_o\|) F_t(\omega_o) $$
